The Jesko Absolut

A TRUE FOUR-SEATER WITH A SPORTY INTERIOR
All four seats all have the same shape and are optimized for comfort with integrated memory foam for ideal support – a feature pioneered in the original Koenigsegg CC carbon bucket seat and the Koenigsegg One:1 seat. The rear seats are as comfortable as the front and the rear space is as celebrated as the front.

EVERYDAY USABILITY
The Gemera comes with eight cup holders (one hot and one cold, per person), plenty of small storage compartments and four reading lights. There is separate climate control for the rear seats as well as the possibility to adjust the volume or change the audio in the car's eleven-speaker system. It is also equipped with tablet connectivity to the onboard free Wi-Fi system and speakers, and induction phone charging in both the front and the rear. All wireless connectivity and navigation needs are addressed.
